Shubh Ki Shyahi

Shubham Mishra is a second-year B.Tech student in Computer Science and IoT from Lucknow, India. Beneath the rigor of code and circuits lies a mind attuned to silence, observation, and the profound depths of human experience.

Writing as shubh_ki_shyahi, he explores the intersections of philosophy, psychology, love, detachment, and spirituality. His reflections question the nature of perception, destiny, and existence itself. Through Hindi and Urdu shayari, he captures the fleeting beauty of emotions, the ache of longing, and the quiet wisdom of nature. Hindu mythology, in his hands, transforms into living metaphor‚Äîparticularly the cosmic dance of Lord Shiva, symbolizing creation, destruction, and eternal renewal.

His work is unorthodox by design: organic, introspective, and guided by the rhythm of imbalance within balance. Nature is not merely a theme‚Äîit is his language. The ink flows freely, embracing uncertainty, randomness, and the quiet rebellion of being an exception.

Shubh Ki Shyahi is more than a collection of words‚Äîit is where inner consciousness meets the free-flowing universe.
